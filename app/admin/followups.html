auto_awesome How Automated Follow-up Works
When a doctor saves a consultation with a prescription, Homatt automatically schedules all follow-up push notifications. No manual action needed. Everything below is configured once and runs forever.
  • check_circle
    Immediately
    Consultation complete notification
    "Your consultation is complete. Prescription ready at [pharmacy]."
  • medication
    Every dose time, every day of course
    Medication reminders
    "Time to take Artemether-Lumefantrine 80/480mg — oral." at 07:00, 21:00 (doctor-set times)
  • sentiment_neutral
    Day 3 (mid-course check-in)
    How are you feeling? [Better] [Same] [Worse]
    Patient taps a button. Better → confirmed. Same → reminder. Worse → auto-escalate.
  • task_alt
    End of medication course
    Course complete check-in
    "Your [N]-day course is done. How do you feel?" Worse → urgent referral sent automatically.
  • calendar_today
    Day before + day of follow-up appointment
    Return-to-clinic reminders
    "Clinic visit tomorrow at 09:00." and "Clinic visit today at 09:00."
