bedtime

Sleep Under an ITN Bed Net

Use an Insecticide-Treated Net (ITN) every night. Mosquitoes are most active from dusk to dawn. Hang the net properly with no gaps and tuck it under the mattress.

home

Indoor Residual Spraying (IRS)

Allow the National Malaria Control Programme sprayers into your home. IRS kills mosquitoes that rest on walls after feeding and protects your whole household.

water

Eliminate Standing Water

Empty or cover containers that collect rainwater — old tyres, buckets, flower pots, and blocked gutters. Mosquitoes breed in still water, especially during rainy season.

healing

Use Mosquito Repellent

Apply repellent containing DEET on exposed skin when outdoors, especially in the evening. Wear long-sleeved clothing when working outside at night.

pregnant_woman

Preventive Treatment in Pregnancy

Pregnant women should take Intermittent Preventive Treatment (IPTp) — sulfadoxine-pyrimethamine tablets — at every antenatal visit after the first trimester.

local_hospital

Seek Testing Within 24 Hours of Fever

If you or your child develops a fever, go to the nearest health centre immediately. Do NOT self-medicate. A Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT) takes only minutes and is free at many government facilities in Uganda.
